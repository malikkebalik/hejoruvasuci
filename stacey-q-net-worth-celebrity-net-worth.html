What is Stacey Q's Net Worth?

Stacey Q is an American pop singer, songwriter, actress, and dancer who has a net worth of $1 million. Stacey Q was born in Fullerton, California in November 1958. She is best known for her single "Two of Hearts" which was released in 1986 and reached #3 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. Her debut studio album Better Than Heaven was released in 1986 on Atlantic records. The album was certified Gold and reached #59 in the US and #36 in Australia. Stacey Q released the albums Hard Machine in 1988, Nights Like This in 1989, Boomerang in 1997, and Color Me Cinnamon in 2010. Her single "Two of Hearts" made the Top 10 in six countries. Stacey Q also had success with the singles "We Connect", "Insecurity", and "Don't Make a Fool of Yourself". As an actress she starred in the movies Cavegirl and Playing the Odds. Stacey Q also appeared in episodes of the TV series The Facts of Life, Full House, and Mama's Family.
