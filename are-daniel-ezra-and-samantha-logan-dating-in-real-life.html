Are Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan Dating in Real-Life?

TV

'All American' fans have wondered if actors Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan have a secret relationship off-camera. Although the two are not an item, Samantha Logan fully supports Spencer and Olivia's relationship on the show.

Published on December 12, 2021

2 min read

In the popular teen drama, All American, Spencer James and Olivia Baker have finally started dating! From the first moment they met and shared a laptop in class, fans have been wanting them together for what seems like an eternity. Since they have such good chemistry on The CW show, many fans have wondered if actors Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan have a secret romance off-screen. So, are the two an item? 

Are Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan dating?

Unfortunately, actors Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan are not dating in real life. While the two have undeniable chemistry on-screen, they don’t have a romance brewing off-screen. The actors have not confirmed an off-screen romance, so it’s safe to say there is no secret relationship happening behind the scenes. But, we can continue to ship Spelivia because they are just the cutest!

Who is Daniel Ezra dating?

At the moment, Daniel Ezra has done a pretty good job at keeping his private life away from the spotlight. Regarding his love life, the British actor has managed to keep that part of his life top-secret. So, fans don’t know if he’s dating someone or not. But for now, it seems the All American star is focusing on his career.

Is Samantha Logan romantically linked to anyone?

While her co-star keeps his love life private, Samantha Logan has not been shy about sharing her romantic relationship online. According to OSSA, Samantha Logan started dating Dylan Sprayberry in 2013, who she met on the set of Teen Wolf. Occasionally, both actors would share pictures and videos together. However, some of their followers are starting to speculate whether or not the two are still together. 

The last time they posted a picture together on their social media was Valentine’s Day in 2019. Since then, they have not posted any other pictures, leading fans to believe they broke up. But, either of them has confirmed a possible split. So, the status of their relationship remains under speculation.

How do the ‘All American’ stars feel about Spencer and Olivia’s relationship?

For Samantha Logan, she simply can’t get enough of Spencer and Olivia’s relationship. In an interview clip posted to the show’s official Twitter page, the All American star opened up about the fan reactions to their relationship.

“The fan reaction has been very passionate,” she said in the 46-second clip. “They are very passionate about Olivia and Spencer. I’m also passionate about [their relationship]. I think they’ve come such a long way since episode one. It’s kind of surreal to see them together and happy.”

Logan continued, “They deserve to be happy. And they deserve to be together. There were definitely a lot of bumps on the way, and neither one of them are perfect. But that’s the whole thing. They’re teenagers, [and] they’re human and imperfect. [But] they love each other.”

All American airs on Mondays at 8 pm E.T on The CW.
