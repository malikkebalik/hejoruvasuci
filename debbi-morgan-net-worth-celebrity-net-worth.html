What is Debbi Morgan's Net Worth?

Debbi Morgan is an American film and television actress who has a net worth of $12.5 million. Debbi Morgan has earned her net worth from her role as Dr. Angie Hubbard on All My Children a daytime soap opera that airs on ABC. She is also well-known for her role on the fourth and fifth season of the TV series Charmed, where she played the character The Seer. Debbi was born on September 20, 1956 in Dunn, North Carolina, her father George a butcher and her mother Lora a teacher. She has a younger sister Terry and she grew up in New York City. All My Children started in 1970 and ended on September 23, 2011. After that Morgan joined that cast of another long running soap opera The Young and the Restless in the role of Yolanda Hamilton. Debbi Morgan has been married 4 times: currently she has been married to Jeffrey Winston since June 2009. Some of her early roles include the 1975 film Mandingo, where she played the role of Dite and the 1979 miniseries Roots: The Next Generations. Her performance as Alex Haley's great aunt Elizabeth Harvey received critical acclaim. She also starred on popular TV shows such as What's Happening and Good Times.
