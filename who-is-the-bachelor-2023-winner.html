Who is the Bachelor 2023 Winner?

Zach Shallcross, originally from Anaheim Hills, California and currently residing in Austin, Texas, will play the starring role in The Bachelor season 27. Zach was one of the three remaining contestants on season 19 of The Bachelorette, along with Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia. As of the September 2022 “After the Final Rose” special for Season 19 of The Bachelorette, Zach has been revealed as the next Bachelor for Season 27.
