What is Brandon Beal's Net Worth?

Brandon Beal is an American singer, songwriter, and producer who has a net worth of $4 million. Brandon Beal was born in Tyler, Texas in December 1983. He is best known for his single "Twerk It Like Miley" which was released through the app Dubsmash and went viral and ended up reached #1 in 14 countries including Denmark, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Beal released his debut album Comfortable in 2008.

His album Truth was released in 2016 and reached #34 in Denmark. His single "Golden" (featuring Lukas Graham) also reached #1 in Denmark. Beal was featured on the singles "Never Let U Go" (Kato featuring Snoop Dogg) and "CPH Girls" (Christopher) which reached #1 and #2 in Denmark respectively. He is signed to the Universal Music Group.
