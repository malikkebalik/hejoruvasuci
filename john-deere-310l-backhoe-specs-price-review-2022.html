Cab comfort

L-Series Backhoes are packed with luxury features, including an efficient HVAC system, a seat that can be adjusted by air or mechanical and an optional premium radio that comes with Bluetooth, auxiliary input, and XM Satellite Radio capability.

Controls

The new “palm-on-top” loader-control grip is even more comfortable and simple to use. Control of the clutch disconnects the automatic proportional roller, and instantly front-wheel drive is all right at your fingertips.

Fingertip electrohydraulic (EH)

Electrohydraulic fingertip (EH) controls of the redesigned pilot levers make for even more convenient use of backhoe features, including the horn and the controlled flow.

Monitor

The state-of-the-art multi-language monitor displays machine diagnostics. Operators can program a range of time-specific features like auto-idle and auto-shutdown quickly and effortlessly.

WorkSight

The only John Deere WorkSight suite of Telematic solutions comes with JDLink Ultimate with real-time machine monitoring and remote diagnostics that maximise uptime and reduce operating costs.

Oil options

L-Series Backhoes are filled factory-fresh with Hydra premium, all-season, anti-wear hydraulic oil designed specifically for construction machines. For colder conditions, choose Hydra XR, which offers all-season protection between -40 and 40 degrees. C.

Maintenance

Ground-level service points on the same side aid in accelerating regular checks and fills. Other features like quick-change filters, longer service intervals, easy-to-read gauges, and simple-to-access grease zerks increase availability and decrease daily expenses.
