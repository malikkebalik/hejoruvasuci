What is Michael Krasny's Net Worth?

Michael Krasny is an American businessman who has a net worth of $1.2 billion. Michael Krasny is a self-made billionaire who secured his place among the world's richest as a founder and former CEO of CDW Corporation. Established in 1984, the company is a provider of technical gadgets, including computers and networking equipment.
