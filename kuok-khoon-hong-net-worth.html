What is Kuok Khoon Hong's Net Worth?

Kuok Khoon Hong is a Singaporean billionaire who has a net worth of $2.3 billion. Kuok Khoon Hong is the Chief Executive Officer of Wilmar International Limited, world's largest palm oil firm in which he is a majority shareholder, while his uncle is holding a one-third stake. The 1991-founded group has expanded over the years into specialty chemicals, as a joint venture with Swiss firm Clariant and with Archer Daniels processed foods.

Apart from running Asia's major agribusiness group, Khoon Hong other investments include a minor share in Zhong Sheng Jian's Yanlord Land and London's Aviva Tower, which he co-owns with Indonesian billionaire and Wilmar shareholder Martua Sitorus. Along with Sitorus, Kuok is investing in property developments located around China's high-speed railways.
