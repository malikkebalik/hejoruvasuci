What is Neal Morse's Net Worth?

Neal Morse is an American singer, musician, and composer who has a net worth of $2 million. Neal Morse was born in Van Nuys, California in August 1960. He has been a part of the bands Spock's Beard, Transatlantic, Yellow Matter Custard, Flying Colors, and The Neal Morse Band. He became a born again Christian in 2002 and started his solo career in Christian rock.

As a solo artist he released his self-titled debut studio album in 1999. His latest solo album Momentum was released in 2012 with moderate success. Morse has been a part of the albums The Grand Experiment and The Similitude of a Dream with The Neal Morse Band, The Light, Beware of Darkness, The Kindness of Strangers, Day for Night, V, and Snow with Spock's Beard, SMPT:e, Bridge Across Forever, The Whirlwind, and Kaleidescope with Transatlantic, Cover to Cover and Cover 2 Cover with Morse Portnoy George, and Flying Colors and Second Nature with Flying Colors. Morse plays the keyboards, drums, and guitar.
