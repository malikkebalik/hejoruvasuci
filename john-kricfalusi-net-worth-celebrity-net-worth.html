What is John Kricfalusi's Net Worth?

John Kricfalusi is a Canadian animator, voice actor, producer, writer, and director who has a net worth of $10 million. John Kricfalusi was born in Chicoutimi, Quebec, Canada in September 1955. He is best known for creating The Ren & Stimpy Show. Kricfalusi also founded the animation company called Spumco.

He was fired by Nickelodeon from The Ren & Stimpy Show in 1992 and started directing and production animated television commercials as well as music videos for artists such as Tenacious D and Bjork. He was also credit with creating the first cartoons to be made exclusive for the internet. He has also worked in television on The Ripping Friends and Ren & Stimpy "Adult Party Cartoon". Kricfalusi has won two Annie Awards and was nominated for Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Animated Programs (For Programming One Hour or Less) for The Ren & Stimpy Show in 1992 and 1993.
