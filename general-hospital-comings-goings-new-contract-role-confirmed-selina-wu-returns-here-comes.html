General Hospital Comings &amp; Goings: New Contract Role Confirmed Selina Wu Returns Here Comes

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that we’ve got some comings and goings news to report, so let’s talk about a popular female mob boss who hasn’t been on-screen lately. If you’ve been missing Selina Wu (Lydia Look), you’re in luck since she’ll return during the week of February 19-23.

Selina is a hit with many fans on social media since she always seems to have a few secrets and ulterior motives.

It’s fun to watch Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) try to navigate his alliance with Selina since he can’t fully trust her, but it’s in his best interest to keep a peaceful partnership in play.

It turns out Sonny and Selina will have a brainstorming session soon, so it sounds like they’ll discuss the mysterious threat that’s been targeting higher-ups in crime organizations.

Selina could be at risk just like Sonny, so they’ll have a meeting and strategize a bit.

We should also mention that Martin Grey (Michael E. Knight) recently left town, so it seems we can go ahead and call that his exit – at least for now.

As for Dex Heller (Evan Hofer), Sonny backed him into a corner and demanded his own departure from the canvas, but he apparently won’t be off-screen for long.

Hofer spoke to SOD about giving Steve Burton (Jason Morgan) a hug while they were both at work.

Since Burton didn’t begin filming until February 5 and won’t start airing again until March 4, it’s easy to connect the dots here and assume Dex will still be appearing in early March.

The show’s credits have also confirmed that Adam Harrington is on contract in the role of John “Jagger” Cates, so it sounds like he’ll be sticking around with substantial airtime.

Other General Hospital spoilers say Eva LaRue has been tapped to play Blaze’s (Jacqueline Grace Lopez) mom, Natalia Rogers-Ramirez, so look for her to make her GH debut on February 26.

Blaze and Kristina Corinthos-Davis (Kate Mansi) have plenty of drama headed their way once Natalia’s plane lands in Port Charles, so stick with us for updates on all the chaos.

As more comings and goings news becomes available, we’ll pass along everything you need to know!
