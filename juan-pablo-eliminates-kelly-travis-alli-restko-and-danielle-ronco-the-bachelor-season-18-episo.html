Juan Pablo Eliminates Kelly Travis, Alli Restko, and Danielle Ronco The Bachelor Season 18 Episo

Juan Pablo started out with 27 bachelorettes on Season 18 of The Bachelor, and there are just eleven contestants remaining. Last night on The Bachelor season 18 episode 4 , Juan Pablo sent Elise and Lauren home after their trip to Seoul, South Korea. According to Reality Steve (who hasn’t been wrong yet about Season 18), on Episode 5, Juan Pablo will send three more bachelorettes packing.

In Episode 5 of The Bachelor, which is set to air on Monday February 3rd, Juan Pablo and his remaining bachelorettes will leave South Korea and travel to Da Nang, Vietnam. After exploring the city of Da Nang on 1-on-1 dates with Nikki Ferrell and Renee Oteri, and a group date with the other nine women, Juan Pablo will send home three of the bachelorettes. According to Reality Steve, the women going home at the end of Season 5 are Kelly Travis, Alli Restko, and Danielle Ronco.

We are about halfway through Season 18 of The Bachelor, so certain women have begun to have an obvious connection with Juan Pablo, and certain women are just disappearing into the background and enjoying their free vacation. It really doesn’t come as a surprise to us that Kelly, Alli, and Danielle will be eliminated during the Rose Ceremony next week, because all three of them seem to enjoy hanging out with each other more than they do Juan Pablo.

We are sad to see Kelly Travis, “Professional Dog Lover,” check out of the Bachelor Mansion though; her dog Molly is super cute!
