Baby will leave the audience heavyhearted: Producer SKN

Baby will leave the audience heavyhearted: Producer SKN

Published on Jul 6, 2023 11:00 pm IST


Baby is being eagerly awaited by movie lovers following the humongous response for ‘O Rendu Prema Meghalila’ song. Starring Anand Deverakonda, Vaishnavi Chaitanya and Viraj Ashwin in lead roles, the film is directed by Sai Rajesh and produced by SKN on his production house Mass Movie Makers. The film is scheduled to open in cinemas on July 14.

In a latest interview as part of the promotions, SKN exuded confidence in Baby’s success at the box office. He said Baby is an intensely emotional love story with an excellent soundtrack. SKN went on to say that there are certain scenes featuring the three lead actors that have never been seen before in a love story. He further said that audiences will come out of theaters with a heavy heart after watching Baby.

SKN is all praise for his director Sai Rajesh and is amazed by his meticulous handling of the emotional story. He also said that his good friends and popular filmmakers Maruthi, Bunny Vas and Vamsi of UV Creations quite liked Baby’s story. SKN said that Baby will be a memorable film in Anand Deverakonda’s career just like his production venture Taxiwaala in Anand’s brother Vijay Deverakonda’s career.

Talking about his future projects, SKN revealed that he will be collaborating with Sai Rajesh for 2 more projects, and 2 more films with Colour Photo director Sandeep and 1 film each with VI Anand and Shyam Singha Roy director Rahul Sankrityan.
