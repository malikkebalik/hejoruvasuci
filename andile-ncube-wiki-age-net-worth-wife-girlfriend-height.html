Blackface Media CEO Andile Ncube Biography

  • Andile Ncube (age: 41, born on October 4, 1981) is a famous TV personality from South Africa whose popularity rose to heights after the telecast of a new Netflix reality show named “Young, Famous & African” on March 18.
  • The show cast includes famous and successful stars of Africa like Khanyi Mbau, Nadia Nakai, Zari the Boss Lady, Annie Macaulay-Idibia, Diamond Platnumz, and Swanky Jerry.
  • This is the first time that a show has represented the African celeb community through the lens of wealth, and people love it.
  • Ncube gained his popularity as a show anchor through shows like “X-Factor Africa,” “The Search: E! Host South Africa Season 1”, “City Ses’la Season 1 as DJ Mphosa”, and “Freestyle.”
