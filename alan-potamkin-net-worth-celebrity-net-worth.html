What is Alan Potamkin's Net Worth?

Alan Potamkin serves as the co-chairman of his father's company, the Potamkin Automotive Group, alongside his brother, Robert. The Potamkin Automotive Group was founded in the 1940s by Victor Potamkin, who passed away in 1995, as a single dealership in Philadelphia. It is now headquartered in Miami, Florida. The brothers took over the business during the '70s and expanded the family company during the '80s and '90s. The Potamkin Automotive Group has since doubled in size from its inception and has dealerships in dozens of locations, including Beverly Hills, Los Angeles, Houston, North Miami Beach, Manhattan, and Philadelphia.

There are about 40 franchise locations around the United States with more than 1,500 employees. The Potamkin Automotive Group specializes in Ferraris, Audis, Volkswagens, Toyotas, and Chryslers.
