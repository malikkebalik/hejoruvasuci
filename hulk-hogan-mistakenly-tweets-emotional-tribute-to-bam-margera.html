Long Story Short

Wrestler Hulk Hogan said he wished Jackass star Bam Margera is "still with us." The good news? He is.

Long Story

Twitter is having a great time right now, at Hulk Hogan's expense.

The legendary wrestler appeared to sent a touching tribute to Bam Margera of Jackass fame.

“Damn Bam I wish you were still with us,” Hogan wrote on Twitter. “I sure would love to hang out again brother.”

But while Margera did a ton of dumb sh*t for our own pleasure in the show's hey-day -- the 38-year-old is still very much alive. 

After deleting the tweet, Hogan put: 

“Got Ryan and Bam mixed up sorry Bam!!! HH.”

Hogan, who is 64-year-old, appears to have mixed up Margera with Ryan Dunn who died in 2011.

Twitter got a kick out of the embarrassing mix up. 


In fairness, who has heard the name Bam Margera since the 2000s?
