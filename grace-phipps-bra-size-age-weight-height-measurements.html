Biography:

Grace Phipps was born on May 4, 1992 in Austin, Texas, USA. After graduating from high school, she moved to Los Angeles to pursue an acting career where she instantly secured the role of Bee in the 2011 remake of Fright Night.

That same year, she won the part of Amy Tiffany Martin in the television series The Nine Lives of Chloe King. In 2012, she gained wider recognition as April Young in the series The Vampire Diaries.

She went on to star as Lela in the Disney Channel Original Movie Teen Beach Movie in 2013, for which she had also contributed solos, including the song “Falling for Ya” for the film’s soundtrack.
