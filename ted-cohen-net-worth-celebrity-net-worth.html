What is Ted Cohen's Net Worth?

Ted Cohen is an American digital entertainment executive who has a net worth of $20 million. Ted Cohen was born in January 1949. As a teenager he frequented The Mike Douglas Show in Cleveland, Ohio and met The Beach Boys, The Rolling Stones, and The Byrds. While still in high school he started managing bands. Ted moved to New York and attended Ithaca College. He worked for WICB, a college radio station and promoted bands and shows. He promoted the Woodstock music festival by giving out tickets.

During his career he has worked in senior management for Warner Bros. Records, EMI Music, and Philips Media. He currently serves as Managing Partner of TAG Strategic. He has worked with artists such as Alice Cooper, Fleetwood Mac, The Who, Van Halen, Prince, The Doobie Brothers, Talking Heads, The Sex Pistols, The Ramones, The Pretenders, Elton John, Neil Young, Stevie Nicks, Dolly Parton, Neil Diamond, The Pointer Sisters, Santana, Dave Matthews, Soundgarden, and more. He is currently chair for the Visionary Committee for MidemNet. He co-chairs the Grammy Technology Committee and works with Grammy In the Schools Program and MusiCares. In 2013 he was selected as the recipient of the NARM's Presidential Award for Sustained Industry Achievement.
