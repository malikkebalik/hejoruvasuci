Meaghan Rath Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Meaghan Rath was born on June 18, 1986 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. She attended Dawson College in Westmount, Canada where she studied Cinema and Communications. Her younger brother, Jesse Rath, is also an actor.

She made her film debut with the 2001 independent feature Lost and Delirious while her first television appearance was in an episode of the series Fries with That?.

From 2004 to 2006, she played the main role of Adena Stiles in the TV show 15/Love. She also became known for other notable characters such as the part of Rigby Hastings in The Assistants. She now stars as Sally Malik in the series Being Human.
