Goldentree Asset Management L.P. 13F annual report

Goldentree Asset Management L.P. is an investment fund managing more than $963 billion ran by George Travers. There are currently 27 companies in Mr. Travers’s portfolio. The largest investments include Bausch Health Cos Inc and Bausch Plus Lomb Corp, together worth $302 billion.

$963 billion Assets Under Management (AUM)

As of 7th February 2024, Goldentree Asset Management L.P.’s top holding is 27,644,959 shares of Bausch Health Cos Inc currently worth over $222 billion and making up 23.0% of the portfolio value. In addition, the fund holds 4,715,829 shares of Bausch Plus Lomb Corp worth $80.5 billion. The third-largest holding is Aercap Holdings Nv worth $72.6 billion and the next is Seaworld Entmt Inc worth $66.2 billion, with 1,252,750 shares owned.

Currently, Goldentree Asset Management L.P.'s portfolio is worth at least $963 billion. The total portfolio value may be much higher due to cash assets that are not publicly disclosed such as fixed income, real estate, or cash equivalents.

Careers at Goldentree Asset Management L.P.

The Goldentree Asset Management L.P. office and employees reside in New York, New York. According to the last 13-F report filed with the SEC, George Travers serves as the Chief Compliance Officer at Goldentree Asset Management L.P..
