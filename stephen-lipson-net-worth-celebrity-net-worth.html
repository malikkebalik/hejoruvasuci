What is Stephen Lipson's Net Worth?

Stephen Lipson is an English record producer, engineer, musician, and songwriter who has a net worth of $15 million. Stephen Lipson was born in March 1954. He has worked with many artists as a record producing including Frankie Goes to Hollywood, Cher, Annie Lennox, Grace Jones, Will Young, Pet Shop Boys, Kelly Clarkson, Hans Zimmer, Jeff Beck, Pharrell Williams, and Johnny Marr. Lipson has also engineered, played guitar, and programmed many of the records that he has produced. Lipson started his career as a guitarist and songwriter and started working as an engineer in 1975. He created his own studio the Regents Park Recording Company in 1976.

He left Regents Park in 1978 and started freelancing. Stephen started to produce in 1983. He worked for Trevor Horn's ZTT Records for seven years. He formed the band Producers in 2006. Their debut studio album Made in Basing Street was released in 2012. Lipson has won Grammy Awards for his work with Jeff Beck, Jars of Clay, and Annie Lennox.
