What is SPLC?

SPLC is the abbreviation for The Southern Poverty Law Center. It is an American non-profit advocacy organization. It specializes in civil rights and public interest litigation. 

The organization is based in Montgomery, Alabama. It is known for legal action against this white-collar lawsuit for targeting hate groups and for other extremist organizations. It also helps in promoting tolerance education programs.

The SPLC’s classifications and lists of hate groups are often cited as authoritative and widely accepted, along with academic and media coverage of such groups. 

These hate groups are those who attack or harm a group of people because of their unique characteristics.

However, the SPLC list has been criticized by those who claim that some of the SPLC lists are foreign, politically motivated, or baseless. 

There have also been allegations of the organization’s misuse or embezzlement of funds, leading some employees to refer to the headquarters as the “House of Poverty.”
